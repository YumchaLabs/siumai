# 2026-05-16

## Opened

Opened the lane after the language extension handle isolation workstream closed. The remaining
compatibility-audit item is hybrid provider composite clients in DeepInfra, Fireworks, and
TogetherAI.

Initial decision: keep the private composite wrappers for compatibility, but make their role
explicit and guard stable family methods against routing through them.

## Closed

Renamed the private composite wrappers to `*CompatCompositeClient`, added a source guard that
checks construction is limited to `compat_language_client_with_ctx(...)`, and updated the
compatibility audit plus migration/changelog notes.

The lane intentionally leaves wrapper deletion for a later compatibility-removal window.
